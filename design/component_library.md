# Component Library

## Buttons
- **Primary Neon Button** – Rounded rectangle, neon cyan glow, glassmorphism background, magnetic hover.
- **Secondary Ghost Button** – Transparent with neon border, subtle bloom.

## Cards
- **Floating Holographic Card** – Glass panel with soft drop shadow, 3D hover lift.
- **Info Panel** – Gradient background, inner glow, anti‑gravity float.

## Navigation
- **Side Navbar** – Semi‑transparent glass, neon accent indicator.
- **Top Bar** – Minimalist, neon underline on active link.

## Inputs
- **Text Field** – Glass background, neon outline, focus bloom.

All components are organized in Figma‑style frames with clear naming: `Button/Primary`, `Card/Holographic`, etc.
